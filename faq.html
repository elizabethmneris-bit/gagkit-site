FAQ

Is packaging discreet?

Yes — plain outer box; no obvious product names.

How fast do you ship?

Orders ship in 1–2 business days; transit is 2–6 business days depending on destination.

Can I send anonymously?

Yep. Tick “Send anonymously” at checkout and we’ll omit your name from the note and label.

What if something arrives damaged?

Email us with a photo and order number — we’ll make it right.
